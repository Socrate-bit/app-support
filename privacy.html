Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ECOM-PARIS LLC ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our applications or related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Usage data (pages visited).
  • Technical data (advertising identifiers such as IDFA).
  • Image data uploaded by users for AI image generation, which may include faces.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services and AI image generation features.
  • To process user-uploaded images solely for the requested image generation task.
  • To respond to user inquiries and customer support requests.
  • To send essential updates and comply with legal obligations.
  • To display personalized advertising and measure ad performance.

3. Advertising and Tracking

Our app uses the Apple Advertising Identifier (IDFA) and may integrate third-party SDKs such as Facebook Ads to deliver personalized ads and measure campaign performance. This may involve tracking user interactions across apps and websites.

Users can control or revoke ad tracking permission at any time through their device settings under Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

4. Face and Biometric Data

Our app does not analyze, identify, recognize, or extract biometric templates from any face data. However, uploaded images may contain faces for the purpose of generating AI output.

User photos containing faces are stored in encrypted form and are accessible only to the uploading user. These images can be deleted at any moment from the user interface.

5. Image and Face Data Processing and Retention

  • User photos are transmitted to Google’s Gemini Nano Banana model for image generation.
  • Google processes the image only transiently and does not store or retain face data or any part of the image.
  • All uploaded images, including those containing faces, are stored securely in encrypted form and accessible only to the user. Generated images are also stored in encrypted form. Users may delete any stored image at any time from the app interface. Images are retained only for user access and support functionality.

6. Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal data or face data with third parties.
  • Third-party advertising partners such as Facebook Ads receive only advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA), not face data.
  • Google Gemini receives image data solely for transient processing. Google does not store, retain, or reuse face data.

7. Third‑Party Face Data Practices

Google Gemini processes image data only in-memory for the duration of the generation task. According to Google’s documented practices:

  • No face data is stored.
  • No face data is reused for model training.
  • No face data is accessible to third parties.

We do not integrate with any third-party service that stores or retains face data.

8. Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect all personal and image data against unauthorized access, alteration, loss, or misuse.

9. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal or image data by contacting us directly. We respond to all verified requests in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any revisions will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

12. Data Deletion Requests

Users can request deletion of all personal and image data associated with their account at any time. To do so:

  • Use the “Delete Data” option available in the app settings, which permanently removes your stored data.
  • Or send an email to contact@ecomparis.org with the subject “Data Deletion Request”.

All verified deletion requests are processed within 30 days and cannot be undone once completed.
